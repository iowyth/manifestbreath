About

I am a multi-disciplinary artist and academic whose practice spans visual art, dance, music, poetry, essays, and textile work.

My work explores the intersections of technology and ancient wisdom, seeking to meld high and low understandings into understated, naturalistic applications in everyday life. As a techno-pagan anarcho-primitivist, I draw from both digital frontiers and primal roots.

Through my various practices, I investigate themes of embodiment, transformation, and the fluid boundaries between self and environment — like water, always moving, always becoming.
