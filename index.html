About

I am a performance artist and PhD student whose research examines participatory culture and research-creation through a métis-crip-queer lens.

My work focuses on building horizontal power relations through community-based praxis, integrating creative expression with social justice and the utopic visioning of radical social alternatives.

I hold a BFA in Intermedia & Cyberarts from Concordia University and am a Joseph-Armand Bombardier Canada Graduate Scholar. I serve as Community Steward for the SORCE Collective.
